Success Story - Transforming SaaS Startup's Engineering Team 

At a bootstrapped SaaS startup, the engineering team was struggling with stagnation, high turnover, and slow delivery cycles amid ambitious growth goals. I stepped in to rebuild the team from the ground up:

  • Team Overhaul: Identified and transitioned out low performers while recruiting and onboarding high-caliber engineers, resulting in a more agile and motivated group.
  • Vendor and Process Integration: Introduced new vendors to enhance tooling and streamlined operations across three parallel workstreams: innovation (new feature development), business continuity (system stability), and tech debt reduction (codebase refactoring).
  • Operational Framework: Implemented the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) to align goals, improve accountability, and foster a culture of continuous improvement.
  • Product Pipeline Ownership: Managed a $1M revenue product pipeline, shifting to a continuous delivery model that reduced release times and minimized downtime.
  • Expansion and Acceleration: Expanded the product line with two new offerings, accelerating development velocity to boost revenue per full-time employee (FTE) to $250K.

These changes slashed cycle times by 40%, reduced turnover to under 10%, and positioned the company for scalable growth without inflating headcount. This transformation not only stabilized operations but also unlocked new revenue streams, proving the power of targeted, AI-augmented engineering leadership for small teams.
